The Durability Paradox

Multi-deliverable analysis of 12 fashion brands across 18 variables examining why avant-garde brands (Rick Owens, Maison Margiela, Comme des Garçons) outlast the brands that outspend them. Includes Python pipeline, Power BI dashboard, 15-slide deck, and written report. Data sourced from OTB Group, LVMH, Kering, and Vestiaire Collective annual filings.

Maison Margiela +23% revenue in 2023 while Balenciaga contracted −15%. Avant-garde brands retain 60–82% of retail value on resale markets. Fast fashion retains 0–5%.
