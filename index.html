BUSINESS ANALYSIS · PYTHON · POWER BI · 2025–2026

The Durability Paradox

↗

Multi-deliverable analysis of 12 fashion brands across 18 variables examining why avant-garde brands (Rick Owens, Maison Margiela, Comme des Garçons) outlast the brands that outspend them. Includes Python pipeline, Power BI dashboard, 15-slide deck, and written report.

Maison Margiela +23% revenue growth in 2023 while Balenciaga contracted -15%. Avant-garde brands retain 60–82% of retail value on secondary markets. Fast fashion retains 0–5%. Scarcity is the product, not a constraint.
